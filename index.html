About Grammar Studios

Grammar Studios is Graham and Amber Losee, a full stack web developer and former director of digital project management, respectively.

With over 15 years of professional experience combined, Grammar Studios was started in 2012. Since then, our team has produced a number of websites for small businesses in Toronto, Ont.

With our expert management skills, technical know-how, lean structure, and professional network, we’re able to deliver valuable results for your business.
